## Description

**1885 $1 MS67 PCGS.** The 1885 Morgan dollar is quite scarce at
the Superb Gem level. This specimen features vibrant mint luster,
brilliant surfaces, and sharply detailed devices. Eye appeal is
outstanding. Population: 69 in 67, 1 finer (3/11).( [Registry values](http://coins.ha.com/c/ref/web-tips.zx#pcgsRegistry): P1, N1793)

**Coin Index Numbers:** (NGC ID# [254R](http://www.ngccoin.com/NGCCoinExplorer/CoinDetail.aspx?UCID=254R), PCGS# [7158](http://www.pcgs.com/coinfacts/coin/detail/7158), GSID# [7494](https://www.greysheet.com/coin-prices/item?entryid=7494))

**Metal:** 90% Silver, 10% Copper

**Weight:** 26.73 grams

**ASW:** 0.77346oz

**Mintage:** 17,787,767
