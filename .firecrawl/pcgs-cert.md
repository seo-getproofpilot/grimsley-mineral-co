## Verify Cert Numbers

Quickly verify certification numbers, pricing, and population data for PCGS-graded coins and banknotes.

Verify

![Cert Page - Desktop](https://images.ctfassets.net/fhzewa1dex84/1NDyswnMleVIV7ruDwhbOA/f02f060568ed48a02907b997e06d4018/certLP-desktopImg.png)![Cert Page - Mobile](https://images.ctfassets.net/fhzewa1dex84/mQwOfHGmdZS9t1RI9wL1T/2b7df60f0375635c4892cf84ef3ec7a9/Screenshot_2025-06-25_at_14.35.27.png)
The Cert # is the number following the slash (/) on a standard PCGS Holder. For example: 8882.65/

25651776

PCGS Cert Verification App

Before you buy any PCGS graded coin, quickly verify its authenticity and quality by scanning or entering the coin's 7 or 8 digit certification number.

[App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/pcgs-cert-verification/id1093919193) [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.pcgs.certverification)

Always Verify Before Purchasing

Verifying a cert number in our database helps mitigate the risk of purchasing counterfeit or altered coins. We encourage the purchase of PCGS-verified coins from our Authorized Dealers.

[Authorized Dealers](https://www.pcgs.com/dealers)
