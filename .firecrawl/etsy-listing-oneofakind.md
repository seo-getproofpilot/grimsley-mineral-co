CUSTOM LAVA LAMP - Stunning One of a Kind Light - Made from a Vintage 1940's Coffee Urn.

READY NOW!

I'm just finishing up my latest projects - some custom lava lamps.

This is hands down the coolest lava I have ever made - I'm only offering it on Etsy.

Check my shop for more unusual pieces. Each lamp is handmade from parts I have available selected from my years of "junk" collecting. These lamps are only available here at Obscure Archive - I can guarantee that!

This lamp is special - It is made from a 1940's Coffee Urn - installed inside is the light assembly and components to hold the lava tube. The glass tube contains silver blue "lava" in a clear solution.

This lamp uses a 40 watt bulb - an extra bulb is included.

It is in nice condition - great vintage look. The lava tube is new - it is a 32oz glass tube.

This is a one of a kind original design.

Overall lamp height is 20" - width is 9.5" at the widest. It has a switch on the side to control the light.

Email me if you have any questions or would like a custom piece. I have quite a few urns, percolators, and cocktail shakers that would made cool lava lamps.
